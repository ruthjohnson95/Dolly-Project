Math 88S: Mathematics & Movies

Spring 2016

This past quarter, I had the pleasure to teach my own course at UCLA. I wanted to teach students that math has so many possibilities, and that the subject doesn't have to be mired in complex computations. I created and taught a course that combined 10 topics from movies with 10 mathematical comcepts. Truly an amazing, unique expereince.

Research in Bodgan Lab
